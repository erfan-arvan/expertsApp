Phase 3: Explain Your Ranking

In this phase, you will explain the reasoning behind your ranking from the previous phase.

  1. Grouped Snippets: explain why the snippets placed in the same position are similarly comprehensible.
  2. Comparisons: explain why the snippets placed in one position are easier to understand than those in the next position.

Tip: There are no right or wrong answers.

Please complete this phase using your own judgment. Do not consult with anyone else or use generative AI tools (ChatGPT, GitHub Copilot, etc.), search engines, or external websites. Your judgment is essential to the validity of our study.
